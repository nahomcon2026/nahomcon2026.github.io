A High-Order Discontinuous Galerkin Method for Westervelt's Nonlinear Wave Equation with Absorbing Boundary Conditions

Track Wave propagation 2

Presenting speaker Lu Zhang (Rice University)

Authors Yiwei Wang (Rice University); Lu Zhang (Rice University)

Abstract

In this talk, we present a high-order discontinuous Galerkin scheme for Westervelt's nonlinear wave equation with absorbing boundary conditions. With simple, mesh-independent choices of the interelement fluxes, the scheme can be made either energy conserving or energy dissipating. We further present the stability and convergence analysis, together with numerical experiments that demonstrate optimal convergence for certain flux choices in both one- and two-dimensional problems.
