Smooth perturbations of diagonally implicit Runge–Kutta methods

Track Time integration 1

Presenting speaker Zachary Grant (UMass Dartmouth)

Authors Sigal Gottlieb, Zachary Grant, Cesar Herrera

Abstract

This research expands upon the mixed accuracy framework for Runge–Kutta methods. Our work develops an accuracy and stability analysis to design methods that strategically replace original operators with lower-accuracy versions to reduce computational costs. Specifically, we focus on designing novel methods that maintain high order for smooth perturbations by satisfying additional local consistency conditions. The paper concludes with a numerical study verifying the performance, accuracy, and stability of these perturbed DIRK methods.
